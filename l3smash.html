You're thirsty, claustrophobic, and scared. The last thing you want to do is sit in this dark cavern and scape two stones together. You're just going to throw it and hope for the best.
The stone shatters, and you see tiny fragments of glass as a piece of paper falls into the water. You reach for it, but it disintegrates almost immediately. You walk back and forth, back and forth endlessly, until you can no longer tell the difference between right and left. Hours or days or weeks later, you drink the murky water out of desperation. You wait for help to come. It never does.
